# Pixel Bloom Logo Reference

## Logo Design
- The logo consists of a stylized flower/bloom icon with "Pixel Bloom" text
- The flower icon appears to be a geometric bloom shape in lime/green color
- "Pixel" is in bold/semi-bold weight
- "Bloom" is in regular weight
- Both words stacked vertically in the logo mark

## Navigation
- All nav items aligned horizontally at the same baseline
- Items: Work, Services, Strategy, About, Blog, Contact
- "Production Hub" is a separate CTA button on the right
- Clean, minimal spacing between nav items
- All items same font size and weight

## Colors
- Background: Pure black (#000000)
- Logo icon: Lime green (#84CC16 or similar)
- Text: White
- Accent: Pink/Magenta for highlights
