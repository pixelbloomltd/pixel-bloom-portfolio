# Pixel Bloom Brand Colors

Based on pixelbloomltd.com:

## Primary Colors
- **Background**: Pure black (#000000 / #0A0A0A)
- **Text Primary**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Text Secondary**: Grey (#9CA3AF / #6B7280)
- **Accent/Mauve**: Purple-mauve (#8B6B8B / approximately)

## Button Styles
- Primary buttons: White background with black text
- Secondary buttons: Transparent with white border
- Accent elements: Mauve/purple color

## Typography
- Clean sans-serif fonts
- White text on dark backgrounds
- Grey for secondary/muted text
